The municipality has a land area of 101.25 square kilometers or 39.09 square miles which constitutes 1.87% of Negros Oriental's total area. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 40,779. This represented 2.85% of the total population of Negros Oriental province, or 0.50% of the overall population of the Central Visayas region. Based on these figures, the population density is computed at 403 inhabitants per square kilometer or 1,043 inhabitants per square mile.

Vallehermoso is a coastal municipality in the province of Negros Oriental.


Location

The municipal center of Vallehermoso is situated at approximately 10° 20' North, 123° 20' East, in the island of Negros. Elevation at these coordinates is estimated at 5.7 meters or 18.7 feet above mean sea level.
